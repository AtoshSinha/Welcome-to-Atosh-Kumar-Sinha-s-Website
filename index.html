Welcome to Atosh Kumar Sinha's Website

Hello! I am Atosh Kumar Sinha, a researcher with expertise in Materials and Manufacturing Engineering.

I earned my Master of Technology and Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering, and I recently presented my doctoral thesis at the National Institute of Technology Durgapur.

My doctoral dissertation is about understanding the effect of process paramerter of WAAM technology on its metallurgical and mechanical properties.

Journal Publications:
